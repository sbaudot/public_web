FOCUS ON: LASER HAIR REMOVAL

Laser hair removal

Smooth skin.
Long term results

Laser and IPL hair removal are long-term hair reduction treatments that use light energy to target and damage hair follicles. This helps slow down and reduce future hair growth over time.
The treatment works by directing light into the pigment (melanin) within the hair. The heat from the light damages the follicle while leaving the surrounding skin unharmed.
Because hair grows in cycles, multiple appointments are needed to achieve the best results. Treatments are timed to target hairs during their active growth stage.
Laser and IPL treatments can be used on both the face and body.

Laser and IPL hair removal are long-term hair reduction treatments that use light energy to target and damage hair follicles. The treatment works by directing light into the pigment within the hair, helping reduce future hair growth over time. Because hair grows in cycles, multiple appointments are needed to achieve the best results.

Laser & IPL hair removal is suitable for many people looking to reduce unwanted hair growth on the face or body.

  • Most effective on darker hair
  • Suitable for many skin types
  • Grey and white hair cannot usually be treated effectively
  • A consultation and patch test are required before treatment

Before treatment, we’ll assess your skin and hair type, discuss your medical history and answer any questions you may have. At each appointment your skin will be cleaned and prepared before treatment begins. Protective goggles are always provided during treatment.

During treatment, a handheld device delivers pulses of light into the hair follicles while protecting the surrounding skin. Some treatments include cooling technology to make the experience more comfortable. Most clients describe the sensation as similar to a quick elastic band snap against the skin.

Mild redness or warmth after treatment is completely normal and usually settles within a few hours. Treated hairs will gradually shed over the following 1–3 weeks. We’ll provide personalised aftercare advice and let you know when it is safe to resume normal skincare products.

How many sessions will I need?
Most clients require at least 6 treatments for best results.

Does it hurt?
Most people find the treatment very manageable with mild discomfort.

Can it treat all hair colours?
Laser & IPL work best on darker hair and are not usually effective on grey or white hair.

How often are appointments?
Treatments are usually spaced 4–8 weeks apart.
